2048 Guide
===============================================================================

* Key-board Controls:
  0. Universal:
    * 'esc' closes the game
    * '-' & '=' toggle dark-mode
    * '/' or 'h' opens this guide
  1. In-game:
    * 'w' or 'UP[-Arrow]' shift the blocks up
    * 'a' or 'LEFT[-Arrow]' shift the blocks left
    * 's' or 'DOWN[-Arrow]' shift the blocks down
    * 'd' or 'RIGHT[-Arrow]' shift the blocks right
